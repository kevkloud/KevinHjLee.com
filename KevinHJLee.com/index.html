About Me

I am a Junior Electrical and Computer Engineering Major at Carnegie Mellon University minoring in Computer Science.

Raised under the influence of GameBoys and MIDI controllers, my passions for technology and gadgets began at a young age. Final Fantasy and after-school cartoons inspired in me dreams of holodecks, hoverboards, and more. Since then, I have developed interests in software development, IoT, embedded systems, audio, and virtual reality. Now, as a student, I am actively pursuing these dreams, and with my experiences as a software engineer both inside and outside the classroom, I hope to one day make them into realities.

Currently, I am seeking opportunities as a Software Engineer where I can apply my skills as a programmer and develop innovative products that will revolutionize the way we use technology.
